Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

PartnerGuru ("we," "I," "PartnerGuru") respects your privacy. This page explains what information this website collects and how it is used. Given the sensitive nature of this work, discretion and confidentiality are taken seriously.

If you use the contact form or email us, we receive the information you choose to share — typically your name, email address, phone number (optional), and a description of your situation. This is used solely to respond to you and discuss whether and how we might work together.

How your information is used

Confidentiality

Any details you share about your business, partnership, or situation are treated as confidential. An NDA can be put in place before any substantive discussion.

This is a static website hosted on Netlify. It does not run advertising trackers. Our hosting provider may also collect standard technical information (such as IP address and browser type) to keep the site secure and running. You may also contact us directly by email instead of using the form.
