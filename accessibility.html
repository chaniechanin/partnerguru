Accessibility Statement

Last updated: July 2026

PartnerGuru is committed to making this website usable for as many people as possible, including people with disabilities. We aim to follow widely recognized guidelines — the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA — as a practical standard for accessibility.

What we've done

  • Semantic, structured HTML with a logical heading order.
  • A "skip to content" link for keyboard and screen-reader users.
  • Descriptive alternative text for meaningful images.
  • Visible keyboard focus indicators and full keyboard navigation.
  • Color contrast chosen to meet AA guidance for readable text.
  • A responsive layout that adapts to different screen sizes and zoom.
  • Support for reduced-motion preferences.

Ongoing effort

Accessibility is an ongoing effort, and some content may not yet be fully optimized. We continue to review and improve the site over time.

Need help or found a problem?

If you have difficulty accessing any part of this site, or you'd like information in a different format, please contact us and we will do our best to help and to fix the issue promptly. Email mendychanin@gmail.com.
