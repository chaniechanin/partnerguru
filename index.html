Questions, answered

What people usually ask

Is this really confidential?

Completely. Every conversation, document, and detail stays private. Discretion is the foundation of this work, and I'm glad to sign an NDA before we begin.

What if my partner won't participate?

That's common, and it's okay. We can begin with just you to get clarity on your position and options. Often, once one person engages thoughtfully, the other becomes willing to as well.

How long does it take?

The Intensive is a focused 10 hours, typically spread over about two weeks — confidential sessions first, then clarity, then a clear action plan. No drawn-out, open-ended consulting.

What if we just need a clean separation?

A respectful exit is one of the most valuable outcomes I help with — separating with clarity, fairness, and the relationship and reputation intact, rather than through a costly fight.

What does it cost?

The Intensive ranges from $15,000 to $30,000 depending on complexity and the number of people involved. When decisions affect hundreds of thousands — or millions — clarity is protection, not an expense.

Do we have to meet in person?

No. The work is done wherever it's most comfortable and discreet for you — in person or fully remote. Clients work with me worldwide.
